# Reliable Fire Protection Services

At Red Rocks Fire Protection, established in 2010, our dedicated team offers unparalleled fire safety services across Colorado. With over 200 years of combined experience, we excel in inspections, maintenance, and monitoring of fire alarm systems, wet sprinkler systems, dry sprinkler systems, antifreeze sprinkler systems, portable fire extinguishers, and more. Under the leadership of Brian Kakac, we ensure that our clients receive the highest level of safety and reliability. ## Fire Sprinkler Services

We offer comprehensive fire sprinkler services, including installation, inspection, and maintenance for all types of systems. Our team is skilled in handling the following services:

 - Mass Voice Evacuation Systems
 - Smoke Control Systems
 - Retrofit and Modifications to Existing Systems
 - Wet and Dry Pipe Systems
 - Preaction and Deluge Systems
 - Standpipe Systems
 - Antifreeze Systems (including residential setups)

These systems are designed to prevent loss and ensure the safety of occupants and property through quick and effective fire suppression. Our inspections and services are aligned with the highest safety standards and regulations.

## Specialized Fire Protection Systems

### Preaction and Deluge Systems

Designed for unique scenarios such as high-value equipment protection, freezing environments, or highly flammable conditions, these systems are vital in specialized fire safety applications.

### Fire Pumps

Fire pumps are essential for maintaining adequate water pressure and flow to fire sprinkler systems in large buildings or high-rise structures.

### Kitchen Suppression Systems

These systems are specifically designed for commercial kitchens, providing rapid suppression in the event of a grease fire.

## Water-based Suppression

### Standpipe Systems

Standpipe systems provide firefighters with a series of valves and hose connections to combat fires efficiently.

## Fire Sprinkler Systems

We offer comprehensive maintenance, servicing, and repair for all fire suppression systems. Regular testing of the interface between fire sprinkler systems and fire alarm systems is critical to ensure seamless life safety coverage.

### Wet Sprinkler Systems

The most common type, these systems, are water-filled and activate upon detection of fire through specific sprinkler heads.

### Dry Sprinkler Systems

Ideal for freezing environments, these systems use pressurized air. Upon activation, the air pressure drops, opening a valve to release water.

### Antifreeze Sprinkler Systems

Designed for freezing conditions, these systems use antifreeze to prevent pipe freezing while ensuring water flow when activated.

## Portable Fire Extinguishers

We service and maintain all types of portable fire extinguishers, including recharging discharged units and performing mandatory 6-year internal servicing and 12-year pressure testing.

## Backflow Prevention Assemblies

Our team provides on-site testing and certification for backflow prevention assemblies. This convenient service streamlines the annual certification process, saving you time and cost. Red Rocks Fire Protection will work with all water jurisdictions to ensure compliance with local regulations.

## Monitoring Services

We deliver 24/7 monitoring for all life safety systems, including fire alarms, security systems, elevators, and areas of refuge, ensuring constant protection.

## Inspections

Regular inspections are vital for compliance and system reliability. By code, fire systems must be tested annually. We thoroughly evaluate and test all components to ensure proper functionality. Comprehensive reports and correction summaries are provided to meet jurisdictional requirements.
