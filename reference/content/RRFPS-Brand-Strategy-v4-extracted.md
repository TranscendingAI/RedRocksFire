## Overview

This Brand Strategy Document establishes the strategic foundation for Red Rocks Fire Protection Services (RRFPS). It defines the company's positioning, messaging, and identity to ensure every communication is aligned, differentiated, and designed to resonate with property owners, managers, and decision-makers across the diverse industries RRFPS serves.

This document will directly inform all future marketing initiatives, including website content, sales messaging, local Google Business Listing, LinkedIn, and other RRFPS business development activities, ensuring consistency, clarity, and growth toward RRFPS's goals.

## 1.0 Company Overview

Red Rocks Fire Protection Services is a Colorado-based, family-owned life safety company established in 2010, delivering comprehensive fire protection and compliance solutions across the Denver Front Range.

We specialize in protecting lives and property through a full-service, single-source approach, serving multi-family communities, commercial facilities, retail centers, healthcare environments, and more.

With a team of highly certified professionals and a proven track record of service since 2010, RRFPS delivers reliable, compliant, and expertly managed life safety systems, built on trust, accountability, and long-term partnership.

## 2.0 Our Story

Red Rocks Fire Protection Services was co-founded in 2010 by Brian Kakac and his father, John Kakac, with a shared vision centered on hard work, accountability, family values, and protecting people through dependable life safety services.

John played a foundational role in shaping both the company and the culture it was built upon. Known for his incredible work ethic, commitment to family, and willingness to sacrifice for the people around him, John helped establish the standards and values that continue to guide Red Rocks Fire Protection Services today. His leadership, mentorship, and vision left a lasting impact on both the business and the people within it.

Although John has since passed, his influence remains deeply woven into the foundation of the company. The principles he believed in—integrity, loyalty, discipline, hard work, and taking care of people—continue to shape the way RRFPS operates every day.

Today, Brian Kakac proudly carries that legacy forward as the CEO and Owner of Red Rocks Fire Protection Services, leading the company with the same values and principles his father helped instill from the beginning. Brian's leadership style reflects a strong commitment to teamwork, mentorship, accountability, and supporting the people around him, both clients and employees alike.

Beyond business, Brian has a strong background in coaching basketball and lacrosse, experiences that reflect many of the same principles his father instilled in him early in life: discipline, teamwork, accountability, leadership, preparation, and showing up for others. That coaching mindset carries directly into the culture at RRFPS, where employees are supported, encouraged, challenged to grow, and treated like family.

From the beginning, the vision for RRFPS was clear: to build a company focused on protecting people, delivered through accountability, reliability, and a higher standard of partnership.

As Brian worked closely with property owners and managers across Colorado, he saw a consistent challenge. Managing life safety systems wasn't just about inspections and equipment, it meant coordinating multiple vendors, keeping up with evolving fire codes, and carrying the responsibility of compliance across complex properties. Too often, clients were left reacting to issues instead of staying ahead of them.

That realization led to the development of a better approach.

Brian built Red Rocks Fire Protection Services around a One Vendor Life Safety Solution, a model designed to simplify compliance, streamline communication, and provide one trusted partner responsible for all life safety systems within a building.

This wasn't just about convenience, it was about accountability.

With one team overseeing fire alarm systems, fire sprinkler systems, fire extinguishers, backflow prevention, kitchen hood suppression systems, bi-directional amplification (BDA) systems, area of refuge communication systems, and monitoring, clients no longer have to manage multiple relationships or worry about gaps in coverage. Instead, they gain a coordinated, proactive partner focused on keeping their properties safe and compliant.

What truly sets Brian and the RRFPS team apart is its commitment to advocacy.

He dedicates significant time to staying ahead of changing fire codes, legislation, and jurisdictional requirements, ensuring clients are not only compliant, but informed and prepared. More importantly, this knowledge is shared proactively, helping clients avoid issues before they arise and giving them confidence in their life safety systems.

Today, the original vision that Brian and John built together continues to guide the company. Their shared commitment to people, service, accountability, and family remains at the heart of Red Rocks Fire Protection Services.

While John is deeply missed, his legacy lives on through the continued growth of the company, the culture of the team, and the relationships built with clients throughout Colorado. Brian and the entire RRFPS team carry that vision forward every day, and there is great pride in knowing the company continues to honor the foundation John helped create.

Red Rocks Fire Protection Services is known not just for what it does, but for how it serves—showing up reliably, communicating clearly, supporting its employees like family, and standing behind every system, every service, and every commitment made.

Because at the end of the day, this business was built on a simple belief: protecting lives and property should never be reactive, it should be proactive, accountable, and handled by a partner you trust.

## 5.0 Brand Positioning Strategy

### 5.1 Positioning Overview

Red Rocks Fire Protection Services is positioned as more than a fire protection company, it is a trusted life safety partner.

This positioning reflects a shift from transactional services to long-term partnership, where clients rely on RRFPS not only for systems, but for guidance, accountability, and peace of mind.

### 5.2 A–C–T Framework

**A – Advocacy**

Advocacy is the foundation of how RRFPS serves its clients.

Led by Owner Brian Kakac, the company stays ahead of evolving fire codes, legislation, and local jurisdiction requirements, proactively educating and guiding clients to remain compliant and protected.

**C – Comprehensive Solutions**

Our Services: RRFPS delivers a fully integrated One Vendor Life Safety Solution, including:

- Inspections
- Fire Sprinkler Service & Systems
- Fire Alarm Service & Systems
- Monitoring Services *(or: "Fire Alarm Monitoring Services")*
- Backflow Prevention Assemblies
- Portable Fire Extinguishers
- Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS)
- Security System Installation & Monitoring
- Kitchen Hood Suppression Systems
- Area of Refuge Communication Systems
- Consulting Services
- 24 Hour Service

All managed through one coordinated team.

**T – Trust**

Trust is earned through consistency, accountability, and follow-through.

RRFPS builds long-term relationships by delivering on its commitments, communicating clearly, and taking ownership of every outcome.

### 5.3 Client-First Storytelling Framework — The Hero & The Guide

At Red Rocks Fire Protection Services, the client is the hero of the story.

Property owners, managers, and facility operators are responsible for protecting lives, maintaining compliance, and managing complex systems under pressure.

They are not looking for another vendor, they are looking for clarity and confidence.

RRFPS serves as the guide.

We simplify the complexity of life safety systems, provide direction, and deliver solutions that help our clients succeed in their role.

### 5.4 Strategic Messaging Shift

Historically, RRFPS has led with services.

This Brand Strategy establishes a critical shift:

- From service-first messaging → to **client-first messaging**
- From "what we do" → to **"who we serve and how we help"**
